Colors

I created a compilation of projects to show the emphasis and power that color can have within design.

Icons of Interest

Color is essential to emphasize the energy of a specific design. I decided to create icons to embody this ideology with my interests and passions. I carefully designed each icon to show the emotion and energy I feel with each of my interests and passions. I created these icons in Adobe Illustrator and made slight typography tweaks using Adobe Photoshop.

Spray of Color

Using photography, I emphasized the emotion and energy of each photo using specific colors. I felt as if action photography was perfect for this, as i could create something unique and that can tell a story to the user that's looking at each image. These photos were edited using Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop.
